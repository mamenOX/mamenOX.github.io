I am an Associate Professor in International Business at the University of Oxford's Saïd Business School. My research investigates the politics of promoting economic development that is both equitable and sustainable. I focus especially on labor regulation in global supply chains and the conditions under which factory working conditions improve. At Oxford I teach in MBA, EMBA, and postgraduate programs and supervise doctoral students. I hold a PhD in Political Science from MIT (2011) and previously taught at MIT's Sloan School of Management. I have been Associate Editor at Industrial & Labor Relations Review since 2018.
